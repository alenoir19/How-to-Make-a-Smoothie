Here are some healthy, tasty, and colorful smoothie recipes!

The Strawberry Banana Smoothie: Makes 2 smoothies

Ingredients

One cup of strawberries, one whole banana, one teaspoon of honey, half cup almond milk or any liquid base you prefer, and half cup crushed ice.

Put all ingriedents into blender until all are well combined.

Pour into desired cups and enjoy!
